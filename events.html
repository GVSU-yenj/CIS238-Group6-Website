Rush Season

Rush season is a fun 2-week period where Sigma Pi is open for recruitment. Recruitment is done through open and social events, and at the end of the period there will be bids placed among participants to join the Sigma Pi Fraternity.

Phantrophic Dinner

Once a semester, Sigma Pi provides the local community a chance to support [ENTER CHARITY HERE], by purchasing tickets to a dinner prepared and served by Sigma Pi. Vegetarians are always welcome. Previous themes have included:

  • South of the Border
  • BBQ
  • Pasta
  • Sandwich & Salads
  • Food on a Stick
